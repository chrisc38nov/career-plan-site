Transportation Inspector

What they do,

Inspect equipment or goods in connection with the safe transport of cargo or people. Includes rail transportation inspectors, such as freight inspectors, rail inspectors, and other inspectors of transportation vehicles not elsewhere classified.

On the Job you would,

Prepare and submit reports after completion of freight shipments. 2. Inspect shipments to ensure that freight is securely braced and blocked. 3. record details about freight conditions, handling of freight, and any problems encountered.
